# Tunnels & Transport

## Tunnel Size
- 5 wide × 5 tall

## Materials
- Floor: Andesite Casing
- Walls: Polished Tuff Bricks
- Ceiling: Dark Oak Slabs
- Corners: Brass Casing
- Lighting: Brass Lanterns or Sconces

## Vertical Transport
- 5×5 shafts at atrium junctions
- Elevators, rope pulleys, or stairs
- Same materials as tunnels
