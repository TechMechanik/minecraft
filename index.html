Building Tips

1

Plan Your Layout

Decide which modules you need before breaking ground. The grid system allows infinite expansion.

2

Gather Materials First

Use the material calculator to stockpile everything you need for each phase before building.

3

Build in Phases

Complete one floor at a time. Test systems as you go rather than waiting until the end.

4

Use Scaffolding

Bamboo scaffolding makes wall construction significantly faster, especially for tall modules.

5

Mark Your Progress

Place temporary blocks to mark sections and layers. Screenshot each phase for documentation.

6

Take Breaks

Large builds take 8-12 hours. Pace yourself and enjoy the process‚Äîthis isn't a race.
